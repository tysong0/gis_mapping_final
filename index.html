Airports and population of US
Travel by air is the default option for many to get accross the country whenever car rides seem to far.
In this interactive map, the location of major US airports with scheduled service is mapped out, together with the county/state population.

Zoom out and click on the markers to explore these ports to the air and their service coverage area, displayed in cyan-blue. (Measured by 1 hour of driving)
You can reset selected airport by click on the Reset selection bar and clear the isochrone area drawn.
Also, feel free to use the location function to the top right to pin your location on this map. Is you home or workplace within one hour driving distance to one of these airports?
